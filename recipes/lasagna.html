Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta dish that consists of layers of flat noodles, cheese, sauce and meat or vegetables. It is typically baked in an oven until the cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbling. Lasagna can be made with different types of noodles, such as fresh, dried or no-boil ones, and different types of cheese, such as mozzarella, ricotta or parmesan. The meat can be ground beef, pork, lamb or sausage, and the vegetables can be spinach, mushrooms, zucchini or eggplant. Lasagna is a popular and versatile dish that can be enjoyed by many people

How to Make Lasagna Step-By-Step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
