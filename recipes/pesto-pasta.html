Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is a delicious and easy dish that can be prepared in minutes with a few simple ingredients. Pesto is a sauce made from fresh basil leaves, garlic, pine nuts, olive oil and cheese, usually Parmesan or Pecorino. It has a bright green color and a rich, aromatic flavor that complements pasta perfectly.

Pesto pasta can be served hot or cold, as a main course or a side dish, and with various additions such as chicken, shrimp, cherry tomatoes or spinach. Pesto pasta is a versatile and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Onion and oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

How to Make Pesto Pasta Step-By-Step

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water and drain
  2. Cook the onion in oil, then stir in the pesto and seasonings.
  3. Add the pesto mixture to the hot pasta and toss with cheese.
